About the Lab Manual

Every lab in this manual is software-agnostic. Whether you are using industry-standard ArcGIS Pro, the open-source power of QGIS, or the accessibility of Web GIS, the learning outcomes are identical.

How to use: Select a lab below, then use the platform toggles at the top of the lab page to switch instructions to your chosen software.
