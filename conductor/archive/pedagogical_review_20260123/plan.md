## Objective
To review and enhance the GIS Digital Textbook, ensuring it aligns with the "Gold Standard" features defined by Dr. Sounny, incorporates rigorous pedagogical strategies, and provides a complete and engaging learning experience for university-level students.

## Gold Standard Features Checklist
- [ ] **Interactive Maps:** LeafletJS integration for spatial exploration.
- [ ] **Auto-graded Quizzes:** Formative assessment in every chapter.
- [ ] **"Local to Global" Sidebar:** Connecting Texas-specific examples to global contexts.
- [ ] **Interactive Data Visualizations:** Charts, sliders (like the EM spectrum tool), and graphs.
- [ ] **"Important Person" Highlight:** Biographical boxes of key figures (e.g., Eratosthenes, Dangermond, Goodchild).
- [ ] **Glossary of Terms:** Definitions for key technical vocabulary.
- [ ] **Summary of Big Ideas:** Concise recap at the end of each chapter.
- [ ] **Geographic Inquiry Prompts:** Analytical questions guiding students through the inquiry process (Ask, Acquire, Explore, Analyze, Act).
- [ ] **"Regional Decisions" Exercises:** Role-playing or scenario-based decision-making activities.
