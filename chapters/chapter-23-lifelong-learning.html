Certifications & Career

Advancing in the profession often involves validating expertise through professional certifications:

  • GISP (Certified GIS Professional): Administered by the GISCI, this portfolio-based certification requires education, experience, and contributions to the field.
  • Esri Technical Certifications: Exam-based certifications that prove proficiency in specific ArcGIS software.

Texas Geographic Information Office (TxGIO)

The 88th Texas Legislature officially renamed the Texas Natural Resources Information System (TNRIS) to the Texas Geographic Information Office (TxGIO). This change strengthens the state's ability to support innovation in the adoption of geographic information systems and promotes the Texas GIS community as a national leader.

The Texas GIS Forum, hosted by TxGIO, remains the premier annual gathering in Austin for networking, career development, and observing how state agencies utilize geospatial technology.
